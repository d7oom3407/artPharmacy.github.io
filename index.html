About Us

Art Pharmacy is a full-service art and culture consultancy implementing creative projects for the public, corporate and private sectors. We engage and collaborate with local artists at all points in their careers, from emerging to established, to deliver bespoke creative projects. As Australia’s first Arts and Culture B-Corporation, we take diversity seriously, not just in our artists but also in the clients we choose to work with and the projects we take on. We provide an end-to-end service, working across the conceptualisation, development and delivery of all art projects. Throughout this process, consultation and communication are at the core of our practice. At every step, we work closely with our clients to ensure that their needs and ambitions are understood. Based on our expert creative and cultural knowledge, we aim to empower our clients to make bold and informed decisions to collaboratively implement unique, innovative and impactful creative visions.







FOUNDING DIRECTOR
Emilya Colliver

Art Pharmacy’s founder and director Emilya Colliver has a long and colourful connection with the art world and after developing a sophisticated eye for art in various prominent London institutions she returned to Sydney and established the Art Pharmacy online gallery and grew it into a prominent art and culture agency committed to supporting the Australian creative community.

GENERAL MANAGER
Brunella Muddle

Brunella Muddle is the General Manager at Art Pharmacy. She works across the spectrum of private to public art projects working with architects, developers, corporate clients, councils and governments to deliver impactful art projects to our communities.

CURATOR & PROJECT MANAGER
Sally Lewis

Sally Lewis is a project manager and curator with a background in fine arts as well as film and television production. Sally has a history of working in the NFP sector, championing artists working with technology in her work for electronic arts platform Electrofringe. Sally additionally produced and presented Carve, an arts and culture radio show on 2SER; interviewing artists working and living in Sydney.

ARTIST LIAISON & PROJECT ASSISTANT
Kit Bylett

Kit brings their unique experience in the fields of architecture, visual arts, graphic design, and communications to the Art Pharmacy team.

Embedded in the Australian arts scene through her own art practice, they are driven to highlight up-coming artists for clients’ projects.

MARKETING & COMMUNICATIONS MANAGER
Ellen Formby

Ellen Formby is a Digital Producer living on Gadigal Land. She was Marketing Coordinator at Red Line Productions at The Old Fitz Theatre, Digital Producer/Board Member of Runway Journal, Producer and Presenter of CARVE Arts & Culture on 2SER Radio (107.3FM) and Artistic Director of Electrofringe, a not-for-profit presenting platform for experimental electronic, technology and performance-based art in Australia.​ In this role, she founded ‘ElectroCities’, the first online residency program of its kind in Sydney, Australia.

DIGITAL STRATEGY
Michelle Wang

Michelle Wang is an art consultant and curator with writing and legal experience, based on Gadigal land/Sydney. She brings a commercial and creative viewpoint to her work handling strategy and operations at Art Pharmacy and it’s sister agency, Sugar Glider Digital.

She previously worked as a lawyer at DLA Piper, and her writing can be found in publications including The Saturday Paper, The Guardian and Melbourne International Film Festival.
